Private by design

Your family's life lives in your own storage and runs on your own AI key. Nothing passes through our servers.

Your storage. Your AI.

Frankly has no servers in the middle. Your records stay in your own iCloud, and every answer is generated by your own OpenAI, Gemini or Claude key. You're never locked in, and your family's information is never ours to see.

Getting your AI key

Getting a key is a little fiddly — sorry about that, and we wish we could smooth it away. We can't, because the key is what keeps Frankly running on your own AI, under your control, with nothing passing through us. It's a one-time setup, and you only need one for the whole family.

Google Gemini Free

  1. Open Google AI Studio and sign in with Google.
  2. Tap Create API key → pick “Default Gemini Project”.
  3. Copy the key and paste it into Frankly.

OpenAI Paid

  1. Open platform.openai.com and sign in.
  2. Tap Create new secret key, then copy it.
  3. Paste it into Frankly. (Needs a little credit on your account.)

Anthropic Claude Paid

  1. Open console.anthropic.com and sign in.
  2. Tap Create Key, then copy it.
  3. Paste it into Frankly. (Needs a little credit on your account.)
