How it works

Who Frankly is for

👨‍👧

For the daily family juggle

Every day is different. Who has training, who needs picking up, what gear to pack. Add it once and just ask, and get a short overview every morning.

❤️

For the people you look after

Keep an older parent's appointments, prescriptions and history in one place. In a clinic, just let the doctor speak with Frankly directly, it answers their questions accurately, in your parent's own words.

🔒

Private by design

Your family's life lives in your own storage and runs on your own AI key. Your family's data never passes through our servers.

Your storage. Your AI.

Frankly is the link between your own AI and your own iCloud. Your records stay in your iCloud, and every answer comes from your own OpenAI, Gemini or Claude key. Your family's content is never ours to see. The only thing Frankly collects is anonymous usage data (which features are used, and whether errors happen), so we can make it better.

Connect your AI

Frankly runs on your own AI account, and it stays on your phone. Three quick steps, once for the whole family.

  1. 1

    Pick your provider

    OpenAI, Google Gemini or Anthropic Claude. Any one works.

  2. 2

    Get a key

    Open your provider's key page and create one: OpenAI, Gemini or Claude. Any cost is on your own provider account, and usage is inexpensive.

  3. 3

    Paste it in

    Paste the key into Frankly once. That is it. Your key never leaves your phone.
